Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 7, 2026

Welcome to Viewora. Viewora is a social media platform where users can create an account, upload posts, shorts, stories, send messages, and interact with other users. By using Viewora, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Address
  • Name
  • Username
  • Bio
  • Profile Photo
  • Posts, Shorts, Stories and Comments
  • Likes and Followers
  • Messages sent inside Viewora
  • Device information required for security

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Display your profile to other users.
  • Show your posts and stories.
  • Provide messaging features.
  • Improve app performance.
  • Prevent abuse and spam.

3. Permissions

  • Camera (for profile photos and uploads)
  • Photos & Storage (to upload media)
  • Internet (to connect with Firebase)
  • Notifications (optional)

4. Third-Party Services

Viewora uses Google Firebase services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Realtime Database
  • Firebase Storage (when enabled)
  • Firebase Hosting

These services process data according to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Information Sharing

We never sell your personal information. Your data is shared only when:

  • Required by law.
  • Necessary to provide Viewora services.
  • Used by Firebase infrastructure.

6. Data Retention

We keep your account information while your account remains active. When you delete your account, your personal information will be removed within a reasonable period unless required by law.

7. Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your Viewora account at any time from the Settings page. After deletion, your account data will no longer be accessible.

8. Children's Privacy

Viewora is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable laws. If we discover that a child has created an account without permission, the account may be removed.

9. Security

We use Firebase Authentication, encrypted connections (HTTPS), and other reasonable security measures to protect your information.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
