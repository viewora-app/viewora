Community Guidelines

Effective Date: July 2026

Viewora is a place where everyone should feel safe, respected, and welcome. These Community Guidelines explain what is allowed and what is not allowed on Viewora.

1. Be Respectful

  • Treat everyone with respect.
  • No bullying or harassment.
  • No hate speech or discrimination.
  • Respect different cultures and opinions.

2. Share Safe Content

  • Do not upload violent or graphic content.
  • No adult or sexually explicit material.
  • No illegal activities or dangerous challenges.
  • No promotion of self-harm.

3. No Spam

  • No fake engagement.
  • No repeated posting.
  • No misleading links.
  • No scams or phishing.

4. Respect Privacy

  • Do not share someone else's private information.
  • Do not impersonate another person.
  • Respect user privacy.

5. Intellectual Property

  • Only upload content you own or have permission to share.
  • Respect copyright and trademarks.

6. Fake Accounts

  • No fake identities.
  • No bots or automated spam accounts.
  • Keep account information accurate.

7. Enforcement

If you violate these guidelines, Viewora may:

  • Remove your content.
  • Limit account features.
  • Suspend your account.
  • Permanently delete your account.

8. Reporting

If you find content that violates these guidelines, please report it through the app or contact us.
